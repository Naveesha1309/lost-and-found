ABOUT US

At Lost&Found, we are solving an age-old problem – easily storing and keeping track of lost and found items for students in the university. Being packed with busy schedule of classes and activities, it is a rather common mishap of forgetting and losing your belongings.
This website is hence to solve this problem once and for all by providing a platform for students to report their lost items and to connect them with those who have found misplaced items around the campus.

Want to know what makes our system so convenient? Here’s what you need to know…
. Easily Submit lost and found items.
. No account signup required.
. Contact the user who’s item potentially matches yours.
. Feature lost items to display on all of our websites and social media platforms.
. Ability to edit your lost or found submission, and delete your lost item entry once found.
. The Website and Mobile Website are all one dynamic platform that is streamlined to seamlessly work together.
